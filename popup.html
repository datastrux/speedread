How to use:

  1. Select text on any webpage (optional)
  2. Choose your preferred reading mode
  3. Adjust speed and display settings
  4. Click "Start Reading"

Tip: If no text is selected, reading starts from the beginning automatically!

Controls: Space = Pause/Play, Esc = Exit, ← → = Adjust Speed, Click Progress Bar = Jump to Position
